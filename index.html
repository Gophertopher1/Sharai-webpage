About Me

Hello! My name is Sharai, and I am a graphic designer. I am a creative professional and am passionate about my work and strive for excellence. I am trained and have skills in design thinking and in telling engaging stories for an exceptional user experience. I graduated from Brigham Young University with a major in Graphic Design and a minor in English Editing. I am also an editor of the English language I would love to work with you. Please do contact me.
